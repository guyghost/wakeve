Wakeve is the easiest way to plan events with your friends, family, or team. Stop endless group chat debates — let everyone vote on the best date and time, then confirm in one tap.

✨ KEY FEATURES

🗓️ Smart Polling & Voting
Create polls with flexible time slots (morning, afternoon, evening, or all-day). Participants vote YES, MAYBE, or NO. Wakeve automatically calculates the best time for everyone using weighted scoring.

📱 Works Offline
No signal? No problem. Wakeve works fully offline — your events, votes, and changes sync automatically when you're back online.

🌍 Multi-Platform
Share events with anyone — they'll have the best experience on their platform with native Android Material You design or iOS Liquid Glass.

📅 Calendar Integration
Add confirmed events directly to your native calendar with one tap. Get reminders and never miss an event.

🎯 Flexible Event Types
Birthday parties, team building, weddings, weekend trips, game nights, and more. Wakeve adapts to any event type with smart suggestions.

👥 Participant Management
Set minimum and maximum participants. Invite friends and let the group decide together.

📍 Location Suggestions
Add potential locations and let the group compare options side by side.

🔒 Privacy First
Your data stays on your device. Wakeve uses local-first architecture with encrypted sync — no data mining, no ads.
