## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

1. **Provide the Service** — Create events, manage polls, sync data across devices
2. **Communicate** — Send push notifications about events you're invited to
3. **Improve the App** — Analyze anonymized crash reports and usage patterns
4. **Ensure Security** — Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local-First Architecture

Wakeve uses a **local-first** architecture:

- Your data is stored primarily **on your device** using encrypted local storage
- Data is synced to our servers **only** when you're online
- You can use most features **offline** without an internet connection

### 4.2 Encryption

- **In transit:** All network communication uses TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)
- **At rest:** Local data is encrypted using platform-native encryption (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore)
- **Server-side:** Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256

### 4.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Active account data | Until account deletion |
| Deleted events | 30 days (for recovery) |
| Anonymized analytics | Indefinitely (no PII) |
| Push notification tokens | Until logout or token expiry |

## 7. Children's Privacy

Wakeve is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 8. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open Wakeve → Profile Settings → Delete Account
2. Or contact us at privacy@wakeve.app

Account deletion is **permanent** and includes:

- All your events (as organizer)
- All your votes and poll responses
- Your profile information
- All synced data from our servers

**Note:** If you organized events with active participants, those participants will retain access to their event copy. Your personal information will be anonymized in those events.
